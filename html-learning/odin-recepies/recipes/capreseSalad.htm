Caprese Salad

Description

Scrambled eggs are a staple for breakfast in many households. They are quick and easy to make, and you can add just about anything to them to make them more interesting. This recipe is for basic scrambled eggs, but you can add whatever you like to them. Try adding some cheese, bacon, sausage, or veggies to make them more interesting.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or cooking oil (for greasing the pan)

Directions

  1. Wash the tomatoes and cut them into thick slices, about 1/4 inch (0.6 cm) thick. Place them on a serving plate or platter.
  2. Slice the fresh mozzarella cheese into similar thickness slices as the tomatoes. Arrange the mozzarella slices on the plate, alternating with the tomato slices.
  3. Take the fresh basil leaves and gently tear them into smaller pieces. Sprinkle them over the tomatoes and mozzarella.
  4. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil over the salad. You can use as much or as little as you prefer.
  5. If desired, drizzle some balsamic glaze over the salad for added sweetness and tanginess. This step is optional but highly recommended.
  6. Season the salad with salt and pepper according to your taste.
  7. Serve the Caprese salad immediately, allowing the flavors to meld together.
