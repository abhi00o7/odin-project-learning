Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a staple for breakfast in many households. They are quick and easy to make, and you can add just about anything to them to make them more interesting. This recipe is for basic scrambled eggs, but you can add whatever you like to them. Try adding some cheese, bacon, sausage, or veggies to make them more interesting.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or cooking oil (for greasing the pan)

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until the yolks and whites are well combined.
  2. Place a non-stick skillet or frying pan on medium heat and add a small amount of butter or cooking oil to grease the pan.
  3. Once the butter has melted or the oil is hot, pour the whisked eggs into the pan.
  4. Stir the eggs gently with a spatula, continuously moving them around the pan to prevent sticking and promote even cooking.
  5. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences while stirring.
  6. Cook the eggs for about 2-3 minutes or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Avoid overcooking to keep them moist and tender.
  7. Once cooked, remove the scrambled eggs from the heat and transfer them to a plate.
  8. Serve the scrambled eggs immediately and enjoy!
